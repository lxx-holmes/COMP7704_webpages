- Gross profit margin rose to 68.15% from 65.56%, reflecting supply chain optimizations and restructuring initiatives launched in 2018 to reduce operational complexity.
- Operating profit margin improved to 24.28% from 19.97% through operational efficiency gains and better management of variable costs like rebates and promotions.
- Return on Assets (ROA) increased to 11.47% from 8.41%, indicating enhanced asset utilization from restructuring and productivity improvements.
- Return on Equity (ROE) climbed to 28.19% from 23.25%, supported by higher net income and efficient equity management amid reduced litigation costs.
- Pharmaceutical segment profitability benefited from divestiture gains and lower litigation expenses; Medical Devices segment rebounded from COVID-19 production disruptions.
- Dividend increases in 2021 demonstrated confidence in sustained profitability and cash flow generation, aligning with improved shareholder returns.
- All profitability ratios (including Return on Sales matching net profit margin at 22.27%) showed year-over-year improvement, highlighting comprehensive efficiency gains in cost control and resource utilization.
