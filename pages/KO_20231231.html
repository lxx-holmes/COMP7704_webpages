Profitability Analysis

- Gross Profit Margin increased from 58.1% (2022) to 59.6% (2023) due to improved production efficiency and cost management, supported by higher net operating revenues.
- Operating Profit Margin declined slightly from 25.4% (2022) to 24.7% (2023) amid rising operating expenses, including increased marketing spending and commodity costs in regions like Latin America and North America.
- Net Profit Margin improved from 22.2% (2022) to 23.4% (2023) driven by higher net income and a reduced effective tax rate (18.1% to 17.4%).
- Return on Assets (ROA) rose from 10.3% (2022) to 11.0% (2023) as net income growth outpaced the 5.3% increase in total assets, reflecting better asset utilization.
- Return on Equity (ROE) increased from 37.0% (2022) to 39.0% (2023) due to higher net income and a slight reduction in equity, enhancing equity efficiency.
- Overall profitability improvements stemmed from revenue growth and cost controls, while operating margin pressure was tied to inflationary expenses and strategic marketing investments.

Liquidity Analysis

- Current ratio decreased slightly from 1.15 (2022) to 1.13 (2023) due to increased current liabilities, particularly short-term debt from additional loans/notes payable.
- Quick ratio declined from 0.77 to 0.72 as cash/cash equivalents fell from $9,519M to $9,366M while current liabilities rose.
- Cash ratio dropped from 0.54 to 0.52 due to reduced cash reserves and higher current liabilities, including current maturities of long-term debt.
- Short-term debt increased in 2023 through commercial paper, credit lines, and debt issuances, raising current liability pressures.
- Operating cash flows remained stable at $11,599M (2023), but higher investing outflows reduced available cash reserves.
- Debt financing activities expanded in 2023, contributing to liquidity ratio declines despite maintained operational cash generation.
- All ratios (current, quick, cash) remain above healthy thresholds, indicating ongoing ability to meet short-term obligations.
- Monitoring recommended to assess sustainability of liquidity position amid continued debt management and cash flow trends.
