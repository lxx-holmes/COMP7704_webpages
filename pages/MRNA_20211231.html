**Risks:**
- Heavy reliance on COVID-19 vaccine revenue, susceptible to demand fluctuations as the pandemic evolves.
- Manufacturing complexities and supply chain risks, including delays in scaling production and third-party dependencies.
- Regulatory uncertainties for vaccine approvals, variant boosters, and clinical trial outcomes across global markets.
- Intense competition from rival COVID-19 vaccines and emerging mRNA therapies, threatening market share.
- Cybersecurity vulnerabilities and data breaches could disrupt operations or compromise sensitive information.

**Opportunities:**
- Expansion of COVID-19 vaccine authorization to pediatric populations and underserved international markets.
- Development of variant-specific boosters (e.g., mRNA-1273) and next-generation vaccines (e.g., mRNA-1283).
- Diversified pipeline targeting infectious diseases, oncology, and rare diseases using mRNA technology.
- Strategic collaborations with governments and organizations to enhance global distribution and production capacity.
- Leveraging mRNA platform for rapid development of novel therapeutics beyond infectious diseases.
