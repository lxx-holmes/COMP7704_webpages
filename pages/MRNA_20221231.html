- Operating Profit Margin dropped from 72.0% to 48.9%, with operating expenses rising to 51.1% of revenue (vs. 28.0%) from expanded R&D pipelines and global commercial infrastructure investments.
- Return on Assets (ROA) decreased from 49.4% to 32.3% due to lower net income and $5.18B invested in marketable securities/property in 2022, reducing asset utilization efficiency.
- Return on Equity (ROE) halved from 86.2% to 43.7% as net income declined and $3.33B share repurchases reduced equity base despite buybacks.
