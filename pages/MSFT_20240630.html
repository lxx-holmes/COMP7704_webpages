- Return on Equity (ROE) decreased from 35.11% to 32.84% as increased equity levels (from retained earnings or acquisition financing) outpaced net income growth.
- Strategic shifts toward cloud services and software subscriptions boosted profitability ratios through higher-margin revenue streams.

- The Activision Blizzard acquisition expanded Microsoft's asset base and equity, temporarily diluting ROA and ROE metrics despite long-term strategic positioning.
