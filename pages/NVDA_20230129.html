- ROA declined from 22.08% (2022) to 10.59% (2023) and ROE from 36.65% to 19.76%, reflecting lower net income and reduced asset/equity efficiency despite slightly lower total assets.

- Profitability pressures intensified from shifting revenue mix, with lower-margin data center growth partially offsetting declines in historically higher-margin gaming/professional visualization segments.
- Foreign exchange headwinds from a stronger USD increased international pricing pressures, potentially reducing demand.
- All profitability ratios showed material deterioration, indicating systemic challenges in cost control and operational efficiency.
