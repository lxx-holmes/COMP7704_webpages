- Gains from property dispositions and development activities contributed significantly to operating income growth.

- Management refinancing strategies reduced interest costs and optimized capital structure, enhancing profitability ratios.
- Notes to financial statements confirm gains from dispositions and lower financing costs as key drivers of ratio improvements.

- Refinancing activities lowered interest expenses by redeeming high-interest senior notes, contributing to improved profitability and reduced financial risk.
- Overall solvency improvements reflect stronger earnings, controlled debt levels, and strategic refinancing to optimize capital structure.
